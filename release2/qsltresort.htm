Beijing Qu Shui Lan Ting Resort

Beijing Qushui Lanting Resort is located in Chaoyang District, Beijing CBD, Tonghui Riverside Cultural Industry Park, Surrounding high-end business offices, cultural and creative industries gather, rich cultural and artistic atmosphere, is the capital city elite favored micro-vacation place.

Beijing Qushui Lanting Resort was officially opened in 2011, with a total investment of 150000000 yuan and a total construction area of 13000 square meters. The hotel is located in the Millennium Bodhi and Ancient Bamboo Forest. The main body of the hotel consists of 4 floors, which integrates spa, guest room, catering, health and other leisure services. The new Chinese style space and soft furnishing design style have achieved the oriental aesthetic lifestyle pursuit of the city elite.

Hangzhou Qu Shui Lan Ting Resort

Hangzhou Qushui Lanting Resort Hotel is located in Qianjiang Century City, the new financial center of Zhejiang and the first-class CBD of Hangzhou. Facing the "3 + 3" industrial cluster dominated by "finance, headquarters, science and technology" and featuring "sports, convention and exhibition, music," it is the first choice for the urban elite group micro-holiday hotel.

Hangzhou Qushui Lanting Resort opened in 2020, with a total investment of 130,000 yuan, With a total construction area of 10000 square meters, the overall architectural style is highly modern geometric, the space design presents the theme of "light" and "water" blend, known as "light and water art museum." The hotel brings together spa, guest room, catering, health and other leisure services in one, fit the ideal of healthy lifestyle of the urban elite, and is a warm space for leisure and entertainment in a bustling international city.
