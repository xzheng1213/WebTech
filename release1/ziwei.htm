Hangzhou Myrtle Restuarant

Hangzhou Myrtle Restaurant is located in Qianjiang Century Park, covering an area of about 1300 square meters, with beautiful scenery and high natural coverage. The overall architectural style has a sense of modern lines. The design of the large floor-to-ceiling glass curtain wall narrows the distance between people and nature, bringing the viewing experience of ultra-wide angle view. The interior space is carefully designed, with high-grade gray tones and customized furniture, less decorative and refined, highlighting the simple and natural texture.

The restaurant is equipped with individual meal bags, boutique tea house, outdoor terrace, outdoor seafood pool, Offering a wide variety of cuisine, selection of high-quality seafood and fruit, all ingredients are selected from the origin of raw materials, to meet the modern urban dining, business, leisure and other high-quality needs of life.
